ABOUT

Mitch Peacock

Owner
Jus Cuz Customs has taken the business from a small shop, to the well known highly recommended shop it is today, based out of Edmonton, AB. We specialize in complete restoration of domestic and foreign vehicles, custom paint, Kandy, pearls, metal flake, flames, custom body kit installation, ground effects, spoilers and complete vehicle painting for that new car look and feel. Although we are a custom paint, graphics, and restoration facility, we are also proud to offer a full range of autobody and collision services. We are a AMVIC (Alberta Motor Vehicle Industry Council) licenced repair facility and insurance claims are accepted.
