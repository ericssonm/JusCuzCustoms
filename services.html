SERVICES

Custom Paint

We offer a full range of custom paint techniques, from Pearls, Kandy, and Flake, to speciallty effects products. Top notch prep work is required to pull off a show quality custom paint job. We have that covered too.

Airbrushing

Styles can range from mild to wild, and nothing is too complicated. Flames, tribal, portraits, murals, and so much more are available. Full color, Black and White, and monochromatic graphics offered.

Repairs

We are a certified and licensed AMVIC (Alberta Motor Vehicles Industry Council) repair facility and all major insurance companies are accepted. We will work with both the customer and with insurance companies to ensure that the repairs are done in a timely fashion to return it to you.

Restoration

Cars and Bikes, Rare and Common. Trust us to take your project to the next level. We offer complete restoration services and specialize in the impossible.
